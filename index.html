About Me

My name is Michael Moreno. I am a front-end web developer with a Bachelors in Psychology from the University of San Francisco with additional study of Java programming and web design at East Los Angeles College. Currently on track to complete a certificate in full-stack web development from the UCLA Extension Coding program with newly developed skills in CSS, JavaScript, and Responsive Web Design.

I am adaptable, self-motivated, and have an eye for detail which I use to create and debug a variety of web-based projects. With my psychology background, I take particular interest in creating websites that adhere to accessibility standards while still creating an appealing client experience. I am eager to continue acquiring new skills, learn from future mentors and team members alike, and leverage my passion for individuals and technical web development skills to develop better experiences on the web.
